More About Me

As a result, I have six years of sales management experience in the financial sector, blockchain, banking, cryptocurrencies, digital marketing, and all other areas of sales.

Everything was great then, until February 24, 2022, when the war broke out in Ukraine, I had to leave everything I knew behind. I suffered so many losses during that time, it took me some time to come to terms with the circumstances, and since I could not find better-paying sales jobs in the Schengen countries, I started looking for alternative ways to increase my income.

After some research, I found that I could have become a developer much earlier. I was still trying to understand web programming, when i met an extraordinary person along the way who eventually became my partner and was also an experienced software engineer. My transition began in mid-2022, during this time I took accredited, paid courses on udemy and a number of other sites, including MDN, to learn how to program. Although it was difficult at first, I eventually managed to finish web development thanks to my continuous learning and programming.

I make it a point to use current libraries and frameworks to create and maintain responsive websites along with my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills.
